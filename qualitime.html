Introduction

QualyTime empowers hobbyists at all levels with the tools and guidance they need to learn, advance, and make the most of their time, offering an array of inspirational materials and expert advice.

Research & Discovery

Interview Research Goals

  • How do people choose, prepare, start, and progress in an activity;
  • What supports people best in their hobbies- materials, best practices etc;
  • When do people feel trust towards information sources in unfamiliar fields;

Research informed hypothesis

Hobbyists need a video-based learning platform, whose content creators serve as teachers and advisors, thereby fostering skill advancement and time optimization.

Research Insights

The 5 one hour interviews with people from the target audience uncovered that:

  1. People feel intimidated by the word “expert”. Calling the app-experts Teachers would make the hobbyists feel more comfortable reaching out.
  2. Reaching out to an online stranger is hard. People would like to know more about the teachers personality and life- provide a Teachers Profile with personal statement, credentials and photos.
  3. People prefer to learn with video tutorials, when possible. Finding the “right” learning materials is so frustrating, that people would often give up early on. Providing expert-proofed video-tutorials created by the QualyTime teachers might help with that problem.
